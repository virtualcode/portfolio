ABOUT ME

Exploring Landscapes Through the Lens and Sails

Welcome to Captured Horizons, where the beauty of landscapes meets the freedom of sailing, all woven together by creative and innovative web design powered by cutting-edge AWS serverless technology. My passion lies in capturing the breathtaking moments of the world's natural wonders and sharing them through an immersive online experience.

Michael Perry


I believe that every landscape tells a story, and it's my mission to capture those stories through the lens of my camera. I'm dedicated to exploring remote corners of the earth, chasing the perfect light, and preserving the essence of nature's grandeur. From towering mountains to serene beaches, my photography transports you to the heart of these remarkable locations.

Sailing is more than a means of transport; it's a way of life that connects me to the vastness of the open sea. I combine my love for photography with the thrill of sailing to access unique perspectives of landscapes often unseen from the shore.
